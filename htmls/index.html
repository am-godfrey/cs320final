Project Summary:

In this project, we analyze the city of Madison's data regarding police incidents and arrests from data released. Our analysis looks into the details of specific crimes as well as general patterns. We hope that our analysis can better advise the police where to enfore their efforts and what the police can do different by showing the trends of crime and how they can adjust.
