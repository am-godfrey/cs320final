Police Incidents In Madison from 2005 - 2020.

The plot below is an overview of Madison, and the prevlance of crime in certain areas from 2005 - 2020.
Darker shades of red indicate places where more crime occurs, and the Black makers represent the 7 police stations.
In this plot, we can cleary see a high prevelance of crime on the Isthmus, into the mouth of the northwestern corridor
In addition, the far Southeast district and south disctrict seems to have an higher prevalnce of crime.
One area of concern for the upcoming future is the safety of the BRT route as the route strongly matches the crime prevelance. 
The increase of traffic around these areas with have increased population and exposure, 
therefore it is in the best intrest of the Police department to plan accordingly.
