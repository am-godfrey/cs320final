The graph above shows how reported crimes are distributed across different times of the day. 
The trend the graph shows is to be expected due the the following reasons.
It can be seen that crimes occur the least between 8:00AM to 4:00PM, likely because that is the time interval with the most human outdoor activity.
Crimes such as theft and drunk-driving are often done when its dark / after late-night parties, which could explain why crime occurs more often during the night.
